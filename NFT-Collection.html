Traditional models for scouting, building and connecting with new talent, have always been one sided. Does the A&R think ​you have what it takes? Does the Label think ​you have what it takes? Does the producer think ​you have what it takes? The true question is: do you think you have what it takes?

The purpose of this NFT is to de-risk talent discovery, increase knowledge of music publishing, and place power back on the artist. While at the same time, building a community of education on how to navigate the current state of music publishing and where it can head with technologies like NFTs.

The NFT acts as an A&R and binds the record label and producer to work to help the artist build a single recording. The contract is an equal partnership for the creative control of the composition. 

Signed and fully executed contract is unlocked with NFT purchase. Just sign, date and email record label. 

Phase 1 - Recording Contract

Recording Contract Includes:
- 1 exclusive instrumental track produced by Wizzo
- 8hr RecordingSession
- Mixing and Arrangement
- Mastering   
- Distribution to all major digital stores
- Administration and Registration [ascap/bmi signup fees included]

Phase 2 - Community Support

Once all 10 tokens have been minted and sold, one token holder will be randomly selected to receive 50% (USD) of their investment as a refund.

Token holders will also have access to mentorship support and will be given access to a token holder only mastermind session occuring once per quarter where artist can ask questions about music publishing, administration, NFTs for music, royalties and all other creative business topics.

Phase 3 - Future Access
All token holders will be whitelisted for early access to future NFT drops and distributions.
